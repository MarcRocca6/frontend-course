Tutorial Solutions

Tutorial solutions are released on Friday evening of the week they were taught in. E.G. Week 3 tutorial solutions are released on Friday evening of week 3.

Solutions are released by updating the README.md in the respective tutorial, as well as adding a solutions folder in that tutorial which contain solutions.

Exercise Solutions

Exercise solutions are released on Monday evening immediately after they are due. E.G. Week 3 exercises are due on Monday afternoon of week 4. The solutions are then made available on the Monday evening of week 4.

The lab solutions are released by pushing a solutions branch to each of your personal lab activity repos.

Issues accessing resources

If you click on a link below, and you receive a 404 message, it means that the tutorial or lab has yet to be released/pushed to your workspace.
