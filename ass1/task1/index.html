HOW WALNUTS ARE IMPORTANT TO UNSW

There are three main types of walnuts found in Sydney:

  • Red walnuts; and
  • Non-red walnuts

Walnuts really can't be oversimplified in terms of how they're eaten. It's actuall very simply. You grab one, you place it on your mouth, you swallow, you smile, everyone is happy.

NUT REGIONS

At UNSW, nuts are grown anywhere that food can also be found. In particular, food courts.

It's important to understand that walnuts have a lot in common with other kinds of nuts and their respective regions too.
